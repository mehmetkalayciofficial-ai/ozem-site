Who we are

Bridging the Gap in Global Pharmaceutical Access.

Meridian operates at the intersection of regulation, logistics, and medicine — a licensed distributor built to move critical therapies across borders where the standard supply chain falls short.

Founded in 2008 by a team of former hospital procurement officers and regulatory pharmacists, Meridian was built to solve a specific problem: too many healthcare institutions in emerging markets could not reliably source the medications their patients needed.

Today we work directly with WHO-prequalified and EMA-approved manufacturers, maintain temperature-controlled warehouses on three continents, and operate under Good Distribution Practice certification end to end. Every shipment is traceable to its batch of origin.

We do not sell to individuals. Our partners are ministries of health, private hospital groups, NGOs, humanitarian agencies, and licensed clinical networks.

Quality assurance

Regulatory Compliance & Quality.

Every link in our supply chain is auditable — from the manufacturer's release certificate to the final delivery point. Compliance is not a department at Meridian; it is the operating model.

Licensed Pharmaceutical Distribution

Holding wholesale distribution authorizations in all jurisdictions of operation, with named qualified persons responsible for every batch released into the supply chain.

GDP & Cold-Chain Certified

Good Distribution Practice certified end-to-end, with 2–8 °C and –20 °C validated lanes, temperature logging on every pallet, and real-time excursion alerts.

WHO · FDA · EMA Source Network

Direct procurement relationships with WHO-prequalified, FDA-approved, and EMA-approved manufacturers. No grey-market sourcing, no parallel trade.

Full Traceability & Batch Documentation

Serialized tracking from manufacturer to receiving institution. Certificates of analysis, temperature logs, and chain-of-custody records available with every shipment.
