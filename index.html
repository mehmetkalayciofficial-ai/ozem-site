Frequently asked

Questions on Ozempic
& Mounjaro supply.

Common questions from institutional buyers. These answers cover supply, logistics, and documentation — not medical use. Consult prescribing information for clinical guidance.

+OzempicWhat Ozempic presentations do you supply?
We supply pre-filled Ozempic® pen injectors in 0.25 mg, 0.5 mg, 1 mg, and 2 mg strengths, sourced from authorised manufacturers. Availability of specific strengths varies by region and quota allocation. Full details shared during partnership onboarding.
+MounjaroWhat Mounjaro strengths are available?
We supply pre-filled Mounjaro® (tirzepatide) single-use pen injectors across titration strengths 2.5 mg through 15 mg. Specific dose availability depends on regional allocation and volume commitment. Contact our partnership desk for current allocation.
+Do you supply to individual consumers?
No. Meridian operates exclusively as a B2B wholesale distributor to licensed healthcare institutions, ministries of health, hospital groups, and registered clinics. We do not supply individual consumers, we do not operate as a retail pharmacy, and we cannot process prescription orders from private patients.
+How are Ozempic and Mounjaro shipped?
All shipments move under validated 2–8 °C cold chain, using passive or active shippers depending on transit duration. Every pallet carries its own temperature logger, and the complete data file is delivered with the consignment. Last-mile delivery is refrigerated where required by destination regulation.
+What documentation comes with each shipment?
Certificate of analysis, certificate of origin, batch records, temperature logs, GDP chain-of-custody records, and customs paperwork. Institutional partners receive the complete documentation package on delivery — sufficient for internal QA and regulatory audit.
+Can we order Ozempic and Mounjaro in the same shipment?
Yes. Combined shipments are common and reduce logistics overhead and customs handling. Our partnership officers structure mixed-product orders based on your institution's program requirements, cold chain lane, and destination.
+Where do you deliver?
Meridian operates across 42+ countries in the Middle East, Africa, Central Asia, Southeast Asia, and Latin America. We maintain bonded cold chain warehouses in Dubai, Singapore, and Panama. If your country is not listed, contact our partnership desk — we likely have a route and can route through our nearest hub.
+What is the minimum order volume?
Minimums are set per institution and per product. Smaller clinics typically start with pallet-quantity orders; ministries of health and hospital groups negotiate larger quarterly commitments. There is no standard minimum — it depends on your programme, destination, and cold chain lane.
+How do we verify authenticity?
Every pen is serialised and traceable to its release certificate from the original manufacturer. Our source network is restricted to authorised producers only — no grey market, no parallel trade, no compounded substitutes. Partners can audit our upstream source chain as part of the onboarding process.
+What is the typical lead time?
Standard lead time is 7–14 days from confirmed order to warehouse dispatch, plus transit. Rush orders are possible on existing programmes with pre-allocated stock. Cold chain and customs add 3–10 days depending on destination and local clearance requirements.
+How do we start a partnership?
Submit the inquiry form on this page or contact our partnership desk directly. After your institution's credentials are verified (typically 2–3 business days), we share a tailored proposal with pricing, lead times, and documentation samples. First shipment is usually within 4–6 weeks of agreement.
+Do you supply to my country specifically?
We operate cold chain lanes to every country in our five-region footprint and can establish new routes on request. "We supply everywhere" is the short version — the honest version is that we supply everywhere our partners hold a valid import license and we hold a valid export authorisation. Contact us and we will confirm for your destination.
